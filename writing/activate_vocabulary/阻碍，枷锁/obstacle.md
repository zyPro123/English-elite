

- **含义**：障碍（需要努力克服的）
    
- **用法**：overcome an obstacle；
- pose an obstacle to；
- an obstacle to progress
    
- **例句**
    
    - Fear of failure is the biggest **obstacle** to success.
        
    - Universities should remove **obstacles** that prevent students from pursuing interdisciplinary studies.

## eg1

Serious food waste on campus [poses an obstacle to] economic development and environmental protection.
