好的，我们直接用 **trammel** 来描述“多能（versatility）的弊端”，来支撑“专精（deep expertise）”的立场。下面给出清晰用法和例句。

---


- **词性**：名词，常用复数 **trammels**，也可用单数 **a trammel**
- **搭配**：
  - **a trammel to sth**.（一种对……的束缚）
  - **the trammels of sth**.（……的种种束缚）
- **语义**：强调“被限制、拖累、无法自由发展”

---



## eg1

> Versatility without depth can become **[a trammel to]** long‑term career growth.

### 2. 用复数（强调“多重”束缚）
> Those who pursue versatility at the expense of depth often find themselves caught in **the trammels of** superficial knowledge — competent in many areas, but master of none.

### 3. 作主语，直接表达弊端
> **The trammels that** a scattered skill set **imposes on** professional advancement are often underestimated.

### 4. 放在对比句中
> While versatility offers initial flexibility, it eventually **becomes a trammel** when deep expertise is required to solve complex problems.

### 5. 更文学化的表达
> A résumé full of diverse experiences but lacking depth is **no more than a collection of trammels** — each skill a shallow tie that fails to anchor real expertise.
